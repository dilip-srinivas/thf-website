Partner With Us

Ways to
Get Involved

The Heritage Foundation welcomes partnerships with individuals, institutions, corporates, and foundations who share our commitment to the Nilgiris.

🏢
Corporate CSR
Direct your CSR mandate toward forest restoration, heritage documentation, or community arts. We provide full compliance documentation and impact reporting.
🌍
Foundation Grants
We welcome grant applications and partnerships with foundations aligned with ecology, cultural heritage, indigenous rights, and community development.
🎓
Academic Research
Partner with us for field research, archaeological documentation, anthropological study, and environmental science in the Nilgiris Biosphere Reserve.
🙋
Volunteer
Join us on field surveys, community programmes, documentation walks, and events. All backgrounds welcome.
📸
Internships
Structured internships for students and early-career professionals in archaeology, ecology, journalism, film, architecture, and community development.
📰
Media & Press
For press enquiries, photo requests, interview access, or documentary collaborations, contact info@the-heritage.org.
